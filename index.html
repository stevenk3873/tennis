About the coach

A patient, methodical approach for every young player.

Max has been coaching since 2006 — guiding everyone from four-year-olds picking up their first racket to teenagers chasing a competitive ranking. His sessions are built around clear fundamentals, steady progress, and genuinely enjoying the game.

Players are grouped by school year and ability so every child is challenged at the right level. Adult and private 1-to-1 coaching is also available on request.
